About

Our Bangalore office opened in 2011 and is at the centre of managing client relationships and conducting
syndicated and consulting research across India.

What makes the Bangalore office a productive, interesting and fun place to work?

The diversity of our staff is vast with people originating from all corners of the country, creating a rich blend
of cultures that come together to solve problems, work hard and have fun.

What unique solutions do you provide clients in India?

We assist our clients across sectors to stay relevant in a highly competitive market. Our client conversations
often centre around measuring key market indicators at city or state levels as well as penetrating markets
beyond our borders. We identify and quantify regional consumer and product trends to provide strategic
direction.
